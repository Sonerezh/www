Sonerezh

My music everywhere

Download (v0.9.0) Try it (v1.0.0-beta)

A self-hosted, web-based application for stream your music, everywhere.

Sonerezh works with the latest web technologies.

Free and distributed under an open source licence.

Preview

Features

Beautiful and intuitive

Enjoy a clean, easy to use interface and modern player.

2-clicks installation

Sonerezh is easy to install thanks to its automatic deployment tool. No headaches with configuration files. Indicate where is your music library, Sonerezh does the rest.

Playlists management

Add titles, albums and artists to your playlists. Listen to them according to your mood.

Share you music

Sonerezh gives you a user management system with two access levels so that your friends could enjoy your music too.
